Reading Tips

Schedule aside 10 minutes of free time for reading each day. Allotting yourself this time will help keep you responsible for your reading and you will eventually work it into a habit that will increase over time.

Don’t set unrealistic goals. Figure out how much you want to read and don’t put pressure on yourself. Remember, reading is all for fun and shouldn’t turn into an internal competition.

Find a reading nook that fits your needs. Finding a space that is the perfect environment or atmosphere for you to succeed at reading is key. Figure out where you read best and stick to it.

Choose books that you actually enjoy. Don’t force yourself to read a book that doesn’t pique your interest or else reading will seem like a chore. Do some research and explore the genres that genuinely intrigue you.
